DigiDuel is where users can battle any desired NFT they hold in their wallets. Simply place your NFT & $DIGI tokens as a bet, and select a color of your choice.

Once the the winning color is revealed, the winner will receive the losers NFT+ the amount of $DIGI that was placed as a bet.

A 1% fee from the total bets will be taken. 0.7% will go towards the staking pool and 0.3% is burned.
Digible has a 10% processing fee for any sale or auction held in the market places. 5% of the total fee will automatically go into the staking pool.

The remainding 5% will go to Digible Operations for the future of Digible, in order for it to be sustainable as well as cover any expenses.
